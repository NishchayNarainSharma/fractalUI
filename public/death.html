The Fractal Nature of Death

Death, like a fractal pattern, repeats itself at every scale of existence. From the death of stars to the death of cells in our bodies, the process of transformation is endless and recursive.

In the same way that a fractal pattern contains infinite copies of itself, each death contains within it the seeds of new beginnings, new patterns, and new forms of existence.
